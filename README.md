# 🌟 Amiwatch  

**Amiwatch** is crafted from the ashes of **Saikou** & **Dantotsu**, blending **simplicity** with **state-of-the-art elegance**.  

It is an **AniList-only client** designed for anime & manga lovers, with support for:  
- 📺 **Anime streaming & downloads** via extensions  
- 📚 **Manga reading**  
- 🔗 Seamless **AniList integration**  

> The name **Amiwatch (আমি দেখি)** comes from Bengali, meaning *“I watch.”*  
> We’d like to say Amiwatch is on its way to becoming one of the **best open-source apps for anime & manga on Android** — and with your support, it just might get there! 🚀
